Bringing Parents and Afterschool Activities Together

StretchKids.com will provide the quickest and most comprehensive way for parents to find afterschool and summer activities and services for their kids. For the providers of those activities, from individuals providing part-time lessons to national chains and franchises, StretchKids.com will a low-cost, effective way to reach parents. How low a cost? The basic listing is free. How effective? StretchKids plans to be the easiest to access, most comprehensive listings available.



Parents

StretchKids will provide parents with the most comprehensive list of activities and services for their children. By focusing on having an up to date and comprehensive listing in their local area, StretchKids hopes to provide parents with more options and better options in a quick and timely manner. As the systems evolves, Stretch Kids will use machine learning technology to help parents find activities they make want but aren’t aware of.



Small

For individual and smaller activity and service providers, including individuals, whether fulltime or part-time, StretchKids will provide a cost effective way to be found be new and former clients. The goal of Stretch Kids is to allow all providers to reach local clients.

Listings are free. As an computer-based service, Stretch Kids will be able to show you how effective you Stretch Kids listing is and offer enhancements to improve it further.

Not accepting new clients? StretchKids can indicate that, and only provide access through an anonymous message feature.



Large

Stretch Kids will provide free listings for larger organizations and businesses that involve more kids, parents, and families and may have multiple locations. Stretch Kids will be an inexpensive additional way to reach their prospective customers when they are looking for activities and services for their kids.
